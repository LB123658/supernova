Galactic Quest

The year is 2127. Humankind failed to avoid climate change and is now facing imminent extinction as the Earth becomes increasingly unlivable. As mass extinctions, famine, extreme weather, and the collapse of society brings us nearer to our end, we must find a way to expand beyond Earth in order to survive. However, only the world's elite possess access to Mars, so you must find the next best life-supporting planet for humankind to move to. But only before time runs out.
